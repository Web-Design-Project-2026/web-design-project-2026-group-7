What is Valborg? 🔥

Walpurgis Night tradition

Valborg (Walpurgis Night) is a Swedish spring festival celebrated on April 30th. Communities gather around massive bonfires, listen to music, and welcome warmer days. For international students, it’s a wonderful introduction to Nordic culture.

📊 Our UX research: 56.3% of international students didn’t know about Valborg, but 87.5% would find a dedicated website useful. That's why we built this guide — to make traditions accessible and fun!

Target audience: International & local students in Jönköping, young adults aged 20-30 seeking events, food, and community.

Did you know? The bonfire tradition symbolizes warding off evil spirits and welcoming the light of spring.
